Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The Carlsbad Chamber of Commerce - Promoting business in Carlsbad, a city of opportunities.

Site Purpose

  • Provide a platform for local businesses to network and collaborate.
  • Promote the economic growth and development of the Carlsbad community.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be hosting this month to encourage business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What are the current initiatives and projects aimed at fostering economic growth in Carlsbad?

Color Schema

  • Main Color: #0066cc (Used for headings and navigation)
  • Accent Color: #ff9900 (Used for buttons and highlights)

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Roboto", Arial, sans-serif (Used for headings)
  • Body Font: "Open Sans", Arial, sans-serif (Used for paragraphs and content)
